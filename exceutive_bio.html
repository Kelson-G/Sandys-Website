Long-Form Bio

Sandy Veautour, Founder and CEO of MindForge Applied Research, LLC, brings more than three decades of experience as a national recognition expert advancing modeling & simulation, systems engineering, and digital modernization initiatives. She has served in senior technical leadership roles supporting the U.S. Army, Missile Defense Agency (MDA) , the U.S. Space Force, and the Combatant Commands, guiding the development of large-scale digital environments, complex M&S architectures, and mission-critical technical solutions.

Her experience additionally spans the MDA Digital Test Enterprise, enterprise M&S frameworks, HWIL capabilities, multi-node simulation architectures, and digital test modernization efforts for National defense advancements. She is recognized for unifying stakeholders, simplifying technical complexity, and ensuring engineering outcomes remain tightly aligned to warfighter needs. She provides senior technical expertise, strategic insight, and mission-focused engineering solutions for complex defense programs.

Sandy founded MindForge Applied Research to provide the nation, our allies, and our partners with elite-level engineering support, strong technical leadership, and mission-aligned architectural expertise that drives clarity and accelerates capability development.

MindForge Applied Research, LLC works with DoD program offices, SETA organizations, FFRDCs, national labs, and defense industry and allied partners.
